# Code of Conduct

Omega follows the [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md).

By participating in the Omega community - through code, issues, pull
requests, discussions, or any other channel - you agree to abide by it.

## Reporting

Report Code of Conduct violations to the project maintainers listed in
[MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). For sensitive reports involving a
maintainer, escalate to the CNCF Code of Conduct Committee at
<conduct@cncf.io>.

Reports are handled confidentially. Reporters will receive an
acknowledgement within 3 business days and a resolution within 14 days
where feasible.

## Enforcement

The maintainer team may, in response to a substantiated violation:

1. Issue a private warning.
2. Issue a public warning.
3. Temporarily ban the participant from project spaces.
4. Permanently ban the participant from project spaces.

Decisions are made by simple majority of maintainers not involved in the
report. Appeals may be sent to <conduct@cncf.io>.
